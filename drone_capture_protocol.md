# Drone Capture Protocol - Inspector Roofing 3D Roof Walkthrough

## Goal

Capture enough overlapping images/video for 3D reconstruction while keeping the roof evidence file reviewable.

## Safety first

- Follow FAA rules and local regulations.
- Do not fly over people when not allowed.
- Avoid unsafe wind or weather.
- Do not capture private areas beyond what is needed for the inspection.
- Blur or avoid faces, license plates, neighbors, and unrelated private details before public release.

## Recommended capture types

### A. Orbit video

- 4K video if possible.
- Slow orbit around the property.
- Keep roof centered.
- Capture 360 degrees around roof when safely possible.
- Add closer oblique passes of damaged slopes.
- Avoid fast yaw and motion blur.

### B. Overlapping photos

- 70-85% overlap.
- Mix nadir/top-down and oblique angles.
- Capture each slope from multiple views.
- Include ridge, valleys, eaves, vents, gutters, soft metals, and damage zones.

## Shot list

- Front wide orbit
- Rear wide orbit
- Left elevation orbit
- Right elevation orbit
- Top-down roof map view
- Oblique slope passes
- Ridge and valley passes
- Damage close-ups
- Soft metal evidence
- Context shots showing slope location
