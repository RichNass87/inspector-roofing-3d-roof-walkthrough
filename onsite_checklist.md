# 3D Roof Walkthrough Onsite Checklist

- [ ] Confirm permission to capture property.
- [ ] Confirm weather and wind conditions are safe.
- [ ] Capture wide property context.
- [ ] Capture full roof orbit.
- [ ] Capture top-down roof reference.
- [ ] Capture oblique slope passes.
- [ ] Capture close-ups of damage areas.
- [ ] Capture soft metal / collateral evidence.
- [ ] Record address/file ID separately.
- [ ] Note limitations: trees, shadows, weather, roof obstruction, access restrictions.
- [ ] Review footage before leaving.
- [ ] Mark whether public demo can be published or must remain private.
